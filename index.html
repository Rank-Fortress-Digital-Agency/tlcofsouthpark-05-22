Our Ocean Project

Read Post
Visit Us

This week we will start our ocean project. First, we will discover what an ocean is and how many the earth has. Then, we will make several lists of what animals live in the ocean, what kinds of plants grow in the ocean and then take a closer look at the different landscape under the sea.

In addition, the children will be learning how to make a pattern and how to make a cube.

Throughout out the project, the children will make a large class ocean mural that they will add to weekly during the project.
